In a post-apocalyptic world, Animalia, a mutant creature, wanders through the ruins of a city. To survive it requires immersion in water, but the sea is out of reach. Director Niles Atallah, an American filmmaker and artist based in Chile, has created an astonishingly rich world, combining visual arts, dance, animation, puppetry and film. Atallah questions how much of ourselves we may be prepared to give to exist in an unforgiving world, whilst exploring class inequalities against the backdrop of a climate catastrophe.
