A bereaved mother journeys across universes to repeatedly kill her daughter’s killer in this lo-fi sci-fi horror.
Irene’s life has only one purpose since she lost her daughter, and no time or space continuum will stop her in her tracks to avenge her loss. Kevin and Matthew McManus have crafted a heartfelt, haunting and truly original film, centred around an incredibly powerful performance from Michaela McManus. When grief and love know no bounds, how far will you go?

Content depiction of child abuse, scenes with sexual violence and graphic violence.
