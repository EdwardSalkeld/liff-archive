Cult director Steven Kostanski resurrects the classic 1980s fantasy in a riotous mix of swords, sorcery and gloriously gooey practical effects.
In the kingdom of Abraxeon, legendary warrior Deathstalker uncovers a cursed amulet that marks him for death from a neverending onslaught of monstrous assassins. To break free, he joins forces with a wizard and a thief to battle the dark necromancer Nekromemnon and his undead army. A triumphant tale of pure fantasy fun and inventive monster creations.

Contains scenes of strong violence and gore.
