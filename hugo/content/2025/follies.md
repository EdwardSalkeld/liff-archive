Eric K. Boulianne’s directorial debut, Follies, is a frank and funny exploration of modern love, sex, and intimacy.
After 16 years of marriage and two kids, François and Julie decide to open up their relationship, diving into swingers’ apps, awkward encounters, and new desires. Boulianne and co-star Catherine Chabot deliver a sharp, sex-positive comedy that balances farce with warmth, offering a witty, relatable reflection on the complexities of contemporary relationships.

Contains sexually explicit scenes.
