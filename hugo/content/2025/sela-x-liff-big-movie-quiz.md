Venue Address: 20 New Briggate, LS1 6NU 
Venue access: Sela Bar is a basement venue accessed via a narrow set of 16 stairs. Unfortunately there is no wheelchair access. Everything at the venue is on the same level apart from the stage which is 18cm higher
Toilets - Toilets are up a narrow set of 17 stairs. Unfortunately there are no toilets with wheelchair access. Gents have 1 cubicle and 2 urinalsLadies have 3 cubicles  Seating - If you need a comfortable seat, or would like to be in a certain section of the bar please let us know in advance to ensure we’re aware of your access needs. We have benches, booths, tall stools, small stools and chairs with backs available.
Parking - There is no parking available at Sela Bar, you can pick up and drop off outside on the corner of Harrison Street. Our nearest car parks are - Q Park - St John’s Centre, LS2 8LQTemplar Car Park, LS2 7PF
Travel guide - Taxis can drop can drop off and pick up on the corner of Harrison Street. Our nearest bus station is Leeds City Station which is 482 metres away. Our nearest train station is Leeds which is 804 metres away.
