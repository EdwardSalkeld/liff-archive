Little Amélie a tender, poignant and visually stunning animated story about the healing power of human connection. 
The world is a perplexing, peaceful mystery to Amélie until a miraculous encounter with chocolate ignites her wild sense of curiosity. Amélie discovers the wonders of nature as well as the emotional truths hidden beneath the surface of her family’s idyllic life as foreigners in post-war Japan. (Little Amélie is rated PG – tickets for under 15s are £6. The film is dubbed in English.)
