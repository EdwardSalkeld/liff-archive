Dark truths emerge from an experimental treatment for memory recovery in this gothic thriller.
Following an accident, Homer accompanies his wife Diana to a remote trauma facility. As fragments of memories return, Diana begins to question what truly happened to her, and how much she can actually trust Homer. An ode to genre cinema of the 70s and 80s, Honey Bunch is as poignant as it is chilling, and features fantastic performances by real-life couple Grace Glowicki and Ben Petrie.
Contains intense and extended use of flashing images.
Contains scenes of graphic violence.
