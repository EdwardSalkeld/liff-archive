A colourful, indigenous folk tale, set amid the coral reefs of the Pacific Islands. Director Ursula Ulmi has drawn on her own cultural heritage and those of her many artistic collaborators from the region to create a film that is rich in symbolism, combining song, costume, pattern, dance and music.
