Sibling writers and directors, Paul and Simon Wade present a complex moral dilemma with honesty and compassion enhanced by an extraordinary performance from actor Toby Jones, a right-to-die evangelist wrestling with his faith when he is asked to assist in the death of a woman who is not terminally ill.
