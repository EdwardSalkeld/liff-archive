Wen, a 17-year-old restless and insecure girl dislikes her disapproving mother, and vows to be nothing like her. She becomes infatuated with her schoolmate Jo, following her down a rabbithole of drugs, sex and dangerous encounters. Wen will discover a part of herself she didn't know existed.
