You may remember Rich Myers' brief global fame when his social media response to Trading Standards banning his use of imported American sprinkles, went viral. Andy uses this fame to boost his Headingley-based dessert shop, in a documentary highlighting the perilous existence of small businesses in an increasingly online world.
