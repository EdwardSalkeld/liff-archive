Based on a real story, recovering addict Joseph starts a job in a Care Home and an unlikely friendship develops with Robin, a selectively mute, autistic resident. After witnessing the vulnerability of Robin, Joseph sets out to encourage him back into a world beyond the confines of his solitary existence.
