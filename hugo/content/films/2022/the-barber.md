Director Dhivya Kate Chetty's first narrative fiction film follows Leyla, a Kurdish refugee, as she waits for her asylum claim to be assessed in Glasgow, her infatuation with a charismatic Turkish barber forces her to confront her past trauma in order to face the future in hope.
